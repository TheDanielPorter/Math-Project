Washington D.C

For Washington DC the goal was to learn more about history and to see cherry blossom trees. "The most popular place to visit the cherry blossom trees is at the Tidal Basin, which provides great photo ops near the Jefferson Memorial, Franklin Delano Roosevelt Memorial and the Martin Luther King, Jr. Memorial."(“Things to Know about the Cherry Blossoms in Washington, DC: Washington DC.”)In Washington DC there are cherry blossoms and several memorials so I can knock off 2 goals at one time. In Washington DC they have a "National Cherry Blossom Festival (March 20 – April 17) is a citywide event that celebrates the 1912 gift of 3,000 cherry blossom trees to Washington, DC by the mayor of Tokyo. This year's festival features a range of spectacular celebrations, including the Opening Ceremony (March 20), the Blossom Kite Festival (March 26), the National Cherry Blossom Festival Parade (April 9) and Petalpalooza (April 16) . Local restaurants even get into the spirit with the Cherry Picks program, while DC-area hotels offer blossom-themed packages, deals and discounts."(“Things to Know about the Cherry Blossoms in Washington, DC: Washington DC.”) They are also several events to go if we go in the spring. Las Vegas

The Slotzilla Zip Line is in the Fremont district. "SlotZilla is a 11-story slot machine-inspired zip line attraction."("Official Site of the Slotzilla Zip Line in Downtown Las Vegas.") There are two types of ziplines in Slotzilla, the Zip-Zilla that"launches from a platform 77 feet in the air. Flyers will be in a seated position and land halfway down the Fremont Street Experience pedestrian promenade."("Official Site of the Slotzilla Zip Line in Downtown Las Vegas.") This is one type of zipline that will help you get from one side of the Fremont district to halfway of the Fremont district while having fun. The other type of Zipline is the Super Hero Zoom which "launches from a platform more than 10 stories up on in the air (114 feet) and whisks flyers 1,750 feet."("Official Site of the Slotzilla Zip Line in Downtown Las Vegas.") This zoomline launches flyers from a higher distance than the Zip-Zilla and has a longer distance. It also sends"flyers prone, or “superhero-style,” for an unprecedented view of the party that never ends at Fremont Street Experience."("Official Site of the Slotzilla Zip Line in Downtown Las Vegas.") Not only will you be able to zipline you can also zipline in a superhero style while looking down at the Fremont district. You can also"travel down the zip lines at up to 40 miles per hour."("Official Site of the Slotzilla Zip Line in Downtown Las Vegas.") Below are some pictures.
